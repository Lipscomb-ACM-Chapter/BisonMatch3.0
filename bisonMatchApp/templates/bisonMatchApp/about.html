About BisonMatch: FAQ, Improvments, Contact Information

So come on, why is this so cheesy?

We went out of our way to add the cheese. Don't take it too seriously.

Who chose these questions?

A couple of computer science and psychology students who had too much time on their hands and terrible taste in memes. Most of these were compiled with some actual thought behind what makes two people compatible, though.

What improvements were made since the last bisonmatch?

We added the fun feature of uploading your picture so that potential matches could see you before they decide to 'take the plunge' and ask you on a date. We also added more free-form general questions (what is your ideal date?) etc. and new ways for you to pay.

Do you really care about my love life?

Only as much as you care about ours.

I'm having troubles with the quiz. Who can I contact?

If you have ANY problems, questions, concerns, or future improvements PLEASE email us at bisonmatch@lipscomb.edu or send us a direct message on instagram @bisonmatch. We would love to hear from you.

What is ACM?

ACM stands for Association for Computing Machinery. To learn more about ACM click here.

We have a question for you!

Can you send us any funny/ interesting dates that come out of this? We LOVE reading your responses and will put up any interesting ones (with names blurred out) on our instagram, or tag us in your posts @bisonmatch. Don't forget to add #bisonmatch!

All proceeds will go to raising funds for LU's ACM student chapter.
